# 🎙️ meeting-scribe - Record meetings and organize notes easily

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Latest_Version-blue.svg)](https://github.com/frenchindochinameningealartery544/meeting-scribe/releases)

Meeting-scribe turns your spoken words into text during meetings. It saves time by creating notes from your conversations. This tool works on your computer, which means your data stays private. You can export summaries to your favorite apps.

## 🛠️ How it works

The application listens to your microphone during calls or meetings. It uses smart technology to change audio into written text. It keeps the text organized. You can search through your past transcripts to find details later. It supports multiple languages, including Ukrainian.

## 💻 Requirements

*   A Windows computer running Windows 10 or 11.
*   A microphone connected to your computer.
*   An internet connection to finish the one-time setup.
*   4 GB of free storage space.

## ⚙️ Setting up your microphone

You must set up your voice so the app recognizes you. Open meeting-scribe and look for the settings gear. Choose your microphone from the list. Speak clearly for a few seconds so the app tests your audio levels. The software uses these levels to catch every word during your meeting.

## 📝 Capturing your first meeting

1.  Open the application before your meeting starts.
2.  Click the record button when your meeting begins.
3.  The app shows live text on your screen as people talk.
4.  Click the stop button when the meeting concludes.
5.  Wait a moment while the app prepares your summary.

## 📤 Exporting your notes

You can send your notes to other applications. Find the export button near your transcript. You can send text to Telegram, save it to Obsidian, or email it to yourself. This helps you keep your notes where you manage your day.

## 🔍 Finding old meetings

The search tool works deep inside your notes. Type a keyword into the search bar. The app shows all meetings that contain that word. This saves you from scrolling through pages of text to find one detail.

## 🔒 Your privacy

This software processes your voice on your own computer. Your audio data stays on your hard drive. No outside service listens to your conversations. You own your notes and your transcripts at all times.

## 📋 Frequently asked questions

**Does the app work without an internet connection?**
Yes. You can record and transcribe meetings while offline. An internet connection helps with the initial download and certain updates.

**Can I delete my old notes?**
Yes. Right-click any meeting entry in your history list and select delete to remove the data from your computer.

**What languages work with this tool?**
The app supports English and Ukrainian. It understands these languages clearly even if people speak quickly.

**Where does the software save my files?**
The app saves all transcripts inside its own folder in your documents section. You can change this location in the settings menu.
